## Attribution

Each character records its artist in its `character.json` `author` field, and the artist
is credited in the dex. When reusing a character's art outside Commitchi, credit that
artist (e.g. "Yuki by @<author>, CC BY 4.0").

The default **Yuki** / **Yurei** sprites are original project art.
